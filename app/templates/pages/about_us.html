About Us

Welcome to Krishi Dibanishi, a platform designed to help farmers make informed decisions. Our mission is to provide useful tools like crop suggestions, weather predictions, and market prices to improve farming productivity and profitability.

Our Vision

We envision a world where farmers have easy access to data-driven insights that help them make smarter decisions for sustainable agriculture.

Our Team

We are a team of undergraduate students from the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department at CUET. Our goal is to use our skills to develop technology that empowers farmers and transforms the agricultural landscape.
